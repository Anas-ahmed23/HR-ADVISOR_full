# GradVoice — Product Context

## Register
brand

## Product Purpose
GradVoice is an AI-powered HR recruitment platform that eliminates manual candidate screening overhead. It combines CV analysis with AI voice screening into one unified workflow — so hiring teams can move from job brief to hiring decision without switching tools or losing candidate context.

## Core Features
- **CV Analyzer**: Parses candidate resumes, scores role fit, extracts skills, flags gaps, generates shortlist recommendations with evidence
- **AI Voice Agent**: Conducts live voice screening interviews with real-time transcription, captures conversational signals, produces structured recruiter summaries

## Users
HR professionals, hiring managers, recruiters at tech companies and agencies. They're overwhelmed by resume volume and inconsistent screening quality. They want speed, signal clarity, and structured output — not demo theatrics.

## Brand Voice
- Decisive, kinetic, surgical
- Direct and evidence-led — not corporate buzzword soup
- Honest about what the tool does, confident in how it does it

## Tone Anti-references
- NOT: generic "AI-powered" SaaS-cream landing page
- NOT: editorial-typographic (Newsreader italic + broadsheet grid)
- NOT: corporate HR tools (Workday, Greenhouse — navy + white + safety)

## Strategic Principles
- Every output should feel decision-ready, not demo-ready
- Signal over noise — structured evidence beats vague summaries
- Unified workflow — CV analysis and voice screening are connected, not separate products
